To: Editor, IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security (TIFS)

Subject: Submission of manuscript "REAEDP: Renyi Entropy Analysis and Evaluation Based (epsilon, delta)-Differential Privacy Protection Method under the Wiener Kernel Space"

Dear Editor,

We are pleased to submit our manuscript "REAEDP: Renyi Entropy Analysis and Evaluation Based (epsilon, delta)-Differential Privacy Protection Method under the Wiener Kernel Space" for consideration for publication in IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security (TIFS).

Scope and contribution. The paper addresses the problem of calibrating and evaluating differential privacy for sensitive data release. We propose a unified framework (REAEDP) that (i) gives an entropy sensitivity bound for histogram statistics (Theorem 4), enabling calibrated epsilon-DP histogram release; (ii) introduces a synthetic-data release mechanism in the Wiener kernel (RKHS) with a formal (epsilon, delta)-DP guarantee (Theorem 5); and (iii) validates the framework with baseline comparison (Laplace vs. Gaussian), ablation studies, and membership inference attack (MIA) evaluation on multiple public tabular datasets. All components are specified in a reproducible algorithm with explicit input/output.

Why TIFS. This work focuses on privacy mechanism design, entropy-based privacy analysis, and secure data release, and is therefore well suited to TIFS and similar privacy and security-oriented journals. The manuscript emphasizes privacy mechanisms, entropy-based analysis, and secure data release with attack-based evaluation (MIA), which aligns with the scope of TIFS. We believe the readership of TIFS will find the combination of information-theoretic bounds, DP mechanism design, and reproducible experiments with baselines and privacy attacks to be of direct relevance.

We confirm that this work is original, has not been published elsewhere, and is not under consideration by another journal. All authors have approved the manuscript and agree with its submission to TIFS.

We look forward to your consideration.
